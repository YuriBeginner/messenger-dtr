OJT DTR Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

OJT DTR Bot ("we", "our", "the system") is a Messenger-based automated timekeeping system designed to record Daily Time Records (DTR) of On-the-Job Training (OJT) students through messages sent to an official Facebook Page.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your Messenger display name
  • Message content (TIME IN / TIME OUT)
  • Timestamp of the message

2. How We Use the Information

The collected information is used solely for recording OJT attendance for academic purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to any third party.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored securely and used only for OJT monitoring and record keeping.

5. Contact

For concerns, contact the OJT coordinator of your institution.
